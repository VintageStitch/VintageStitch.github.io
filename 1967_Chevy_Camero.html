Conveniently located in-between the Greater Milwaukee and Chicagoland area. Our shop is right on the Wisconsin and Illinois border in Bristol, Wisconsin.

Established back in 1985, Vintage Stitch is an automotive interior and top specialist that services automotive interiors, custom tops, and any restoration work needed to be done to your vehicle. For automotive interiors, we service headliners, seats, door panels, carpets, and more. For custom tops, we service vinyl tops, convertible tops, and custom-made tops. Our restoration services include upholstery, embroidery, glass, and wiring.

Throughout our 35+ years, we have worked on most American and foreign vehicles such as Chevy, Dodge, Ford, Toyota, Honda, Audi, Lexus, BMW, Jeep, and many more. Our work has been featured in showcases such as SEMA and the Hot Wheels Legend Tour.

Vintage Stitch’s mission is to provide high quality work and customer service while transforming your car’s dream into a reality. Whether it’s putting a new top on your convertible or changing the interior in your car, we will work with you to ensure your expectations are met. Throughout the whole servicing process, you can be sure that your car will be handled with care.

Two things Vintage Stitch takes seriously with our customers are honesty and transparency. We strongly believe in educating our customers on the work we have done to their car and keeping them up-to-date while working on their vehicle. By doing so, we have earned the trust of customers all over the Greater Milwaukee and Chicagoland area.

About Us

Vintage Stitch is a automotive interior and top specialist located in Bristol, Wisconsin that has served the Greater Milwaukee and Chicagoland area for 35+ years. From roof to the floor and everything in-between, we have the tools and knowledge to fulfill you and your car's dreams.
